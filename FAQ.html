Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is neuroentrepreneurship?
A: It is an improvement to the already existing field of entrepreneurship. The difference is that it will: 1. Simplify and apply brain and behavior based principles in order to improve startup performance and wellbeing. 2. Target impact and service 3. Imply a holistic approach in which sustainable business practices, social responsibility and employee/community wellbeing are prioritized.
Q: What can it address wellbeing-wise?
A: Founder's depression, decision-making, decision-fatigue, stress, blindspots, reasons of failure, etc. - they also start and/or take place in the brain.
Q: How about performance-wise?
A: New/improved customer relationships, team dynamics, retention, negotiation, leadership, startup culture, and many more. Once you understand your brain, you'll be able to better understand other people's behavior as well.
Q: "How does this become a billion dollar company?"
